BoroBlast

BoroBlast is an annual state-level qualifier for the FLL hosted by Team 75, RoboRaiders. In this competition, FLL, Junior FLL, and FTC teams participate in a variety of different Robotics and STEM-related events that challenge them to think innovatively and come up with a solution that satisfies all aspects of the problem presented to them.
