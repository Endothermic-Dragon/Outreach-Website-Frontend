What is FIRST?

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global organization that aims to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders. FIRST offers a variety of challenging programs for students of different ages, including the FIRST Robotics Competition, FIRST Tech Challenge, and FIRST LEGO League.

Through these programs, students work in teams to design, build, and program robots to compete in exciting challenges. They also learn important skills such as problem-solving, teamwork, leadership, and communication, while gaining hands-on experience in engineering, coding, and other STEM fields.

In addition to the technical skills, FIRST also emphasizes core values such as inclusivity, respect, and professionalism. It encourages students to develop a passion for STEM and pursue careers in these fields, while fostering a community of like-minded individuals who share a love for science and technology.
