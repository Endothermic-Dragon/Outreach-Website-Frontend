Privacy Policy

All users:
  • Users are authenticated via a server-generated token assigned on sign in.
    • This token expires after 1.5 hours.
  • The Google Sign In button at the bottom left allows the server to access your name and profile. It then generates a token associating the client (you) with your profile data on the server.
    • Your Google Account ID and Google access tokens never leave the server.
  • All users with the mentor tag have access to the profile of all users. This means that they can view any and all hours that you log, along with your name, email, and profile picture.
