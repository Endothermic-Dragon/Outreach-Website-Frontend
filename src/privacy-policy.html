Privacy Policy

  • When you sign in to this app:
    • Access: we collect your name and email address from your Google account.
    • Storage: this data is stored in our encrypted database in the backend.
    • Use: our database cross-checks to make sure your details match when logging in.
    • Sharing: we do not share your information with any other application. However, application admins (mentors or Student Leadership in our warehouse) will be able to see these details. But they already know this information from when you joined Team 75, RoboRaiders!
  • When logging in, we ensure that you are an authorized user approved by and whitelisted by Team 75's mentors. If you are not, your login attempt will be blocked. Please contact debnathe@htps.us to get your email approved.
  • After a successful login, you are assigned a session that automatically expires after 1.5 hours.
    • Please note that we store a session identifier locally on your computer. This is essential to website functionality to prevent unauthorized individuals from gaining access to private data.
